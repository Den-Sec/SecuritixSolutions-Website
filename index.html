Our Story

Progetto Cybersecurity is a professional agency providing cybersecurity services, training, antivirus installation, analysis, and much more for SMEs and large distribution in Italy. Our origins are rooted in the passion and experience of a cybersecurity expert who studied at top institutions in the United States and has become one of the most qualified professionals in Italy. Our competitive edge lies in the quality of our services and the expertise of our team. We offer a comprehensive range of security services, from risk assessments to full-scale security management, ensuring maximum protection for the companies we work with.

Founded in 2020, we have been dedicated to protecting businesses of all sizes. Our expert team has consistently provided top-notch cybersecurity solutions, training, and consultations, empowering our clients to navigate the digital landscape securely.
