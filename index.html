Protect Your Business from Costly Cyber Threats

According to IBM's Cost of a Data Breach Report 2023, the average cost of a cyber attack was $4.45 million globally. These devastating attacks result in data loss, downtime, legal expenses, and fines for non-compliance with data protection regulations. Global losses were estimated at $8 trillion in 2023, with a projected increase to $9.5 trillion in 2024.

Every company, regardless of size or industry, can be hit by a cyber attack. The consequences can be catastrophic: loss of customer trust, irreparable reputational damage, and severe financial repercussions. No one is immune, and the threat is constantly increasing. Many cybersecurity companies offer software solutions that require in-house management, adding further complexity for already pressured businesses. These software solutions often need continuous updates, constant monitoring, and qualified personnel, leaving clients with a complex and burdensome task.

SecuritiX is the Solution! Instead of selling software for self-management, we offer a fully managed service, ensuring maximum data protection without the burden of software administration. Our solution eliminates complications, allowing clients to focus on their core business while we handle their cybersecurity needs.

Our Story

Our adventure began in 2016, founded on a solid friendship formed in school and a common aspiration to grow and innovate in the field of technology. Dennis pursued academic studies in the United States, honing his technical and managerial skills in cybersecurity with a degree in Cyber Security, certifications from CISCO, CompTIA, and Amazon Web Services. Outside the academic path, he participated in national-level competitions in the US, solidifying his position as a leader in the sector, including the most important: the National Cyber League, ranked in the Top 7% in 2023, and the Mid-Atlantic Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition, placing 16th in 2023.

Meanwhile, Marco built his career in Italy, specializing in marketing, business, and communication. With his training and acquired experience, Marco became a leader in business management, significantly contributing to the success of the organizations he worked for. His strategic vision and leadership skills make him a fundamental pillar for growth and innovation.

After years of intense training and professional development, we decided to combine our skills to create SecuritiX. Our mission is to offer highly efficient and transparent cybersecurity services, ensuring our clients the maximum protection of their data. With our strong experience and dedication, clients can rely on us to create and maintain a secure and compliant digital ecosystem.
